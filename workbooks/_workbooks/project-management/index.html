--- title: "09: Project Management" layout: single author: Andrew Severin header: overlay_image: 09-project-management/assets/img/09_project_management_banner.png org: 1 ordered: 9 wbs: data-science description: Describe the workbook for index and search purposes --- It is undeniable that good project management leads to improved reproducibility and research productivity. Project management becomes significantly more important as the size of the project and the amount of data increases. Project management is not just about how you organize your data, files and folders but also how you record the steps in data analysis that leads to the publication of your research.
